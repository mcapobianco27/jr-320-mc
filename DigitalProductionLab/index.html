Milan/Cortina: The Host of the 2026 Olympic Games

By Michael Capobianco

The 2026 Olympic Winter Games are set to kick off on Friday evening at the San Siro Stadium in Milan, Italy. The 25th rendition of the legendary event will take place across Northern Italy, with Milan and the village of Cortina d'Ampezzo in the Italian Dolomites mountain range being the two main hosts.
