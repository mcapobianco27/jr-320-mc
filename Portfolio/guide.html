2026 Team Previews
Arizona Diamondbacks

The Arizona Diamondbacks enter the 2026 season as a dark-horse playoff contender barring major injuries. Coming off of a disappointing 80-82 2025 campaign, the Snakes will look to find themselves back in postseason play. Veteran third baseman Nolan Arenado was added to the lineup over the offseason, which has the potential to be potent, headlined by Corbin Carroll, Geraldo Perdomo and Ketel Marte, the latter of whom signed a six-year, $116.5 million extension last April. If Merrill Kelly and Ryne Nelson can hold things down on the mound to start the season, the return of star pitcher Corbin Burnes would be extra-sweet.

  • 2025 Record: 80-82 (4th in NL West), missed playoffs
  • Player to Watch: Ryne Nelson
  • 2026 Projected Win Total per Fangraphs: 81.6 (3rd in NL West)
